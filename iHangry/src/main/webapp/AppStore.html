Usage

iHangry is a tool to help you locate a quick place to grab a bite to eat. When you're on the go and you need a quick meal iHangry pulls up whats close and helps solve your hunger needs by locating great places to eat!

The first page that you are presented is the location finder. You can allow your GPS coordinates to be sent to our app and it will then locate and display on the map a list of the closest resturants to your location. You can also manually enter in an address or general location and it will use that information to pull up places to eat.

Enjoy your meal and solve that iHangry!
